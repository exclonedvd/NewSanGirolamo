Breakfast

Every morning we serve Mantuan-style breakfasts: homemade cakes, tarts with garden jams, fresh bread, honey from our apiary, yogurt, seasonal fruit, and a selection of freshly prepared hot drinks. Simple, authentic, and carefully selected products, for a sweet start to the day.

Dining

For refreshments, we offer platters of typical local cured meats and cheeses, accompanied by homemade giardiniera, Mantua mustards, local bread, and select oils. An authentic break showcasing the flavors of the countryside and the excellence of small producers.
